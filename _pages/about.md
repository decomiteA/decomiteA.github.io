I am a post-doctoral fellow in the [Motor Control group](https://www.seethapathilab.org/) (headed by Prof. Nidhi Seethapathi) at the [McGovern Institute for Brain Science](https://mcgovern.mit.edu/) at MIT. I am supported by the [K. Lisa Yang Integrative Computational Neuroscience (IcON) center](https://yangtan.mit.edu/icon/). I completed my PhD at UCLouvain working with [Philippe Lefevre](https://perso.uclouvain.be/philippe.lefevre/) and Frederic Crevecoeur.

My research investigates the principles underlying motor control during naturalist tasks (reaching, locomotion, manipulation, ...) across species. I am using a combination of experimental (open field recording, marker-less and marker-based motion capture) and modeling (optimal control, deep reinforcement learning, controller inference).
